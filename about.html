About This Project

This site was developed as a Computer Science assignment to demonstrate the creation of a clean, responsive static web application. I chose Elden Ring as the theme because of the cool character design and the complex lore created by FromSoftware and George R.R. Martin.

The gallery highlights key figures in the Lands Between, from the eternal Queen Marika to the tragic Malenia.
