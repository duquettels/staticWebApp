Backstory

Queen Marika the Eternal is the sovereign of the Lands Between and the mother of the demigods. As an Empyrean, she was chosen by the Greater Will to become the vessel for the Elden Ring, establishing the Golden Order that ruled the world for ages.

Abilities & Role

"The Queen's sorrow was great, but her ambition was greater."

  • Divine Power: Capability to shatter or reform the Elden Ring.
  • Golden Grace: Bestows or removes the "Grace" from the inhabitants of the Lands Between.
  • Dual Nature: Exists in a mysterious, physical duality with Radagon of the Golden Order.

Personality

Enigmatic and calculating, Marika's personality shifted from a faithful tool of the Greater Will to a rebellious figure who ultimately shattered the Ring, plunging the world into the Shattering.
